Born in South Africa, Built for Change

Montelo Solar was founded in 2023 by a team of South African energy enthusiasts who saw the need for a transparent, accessible platform to connect homeowners and businesses with reliable solar solutions.

Our mission is to empower South Africans to take control of their energy needs while supporting the growth of local solar businesses and driving awareness in both urban and rural communities.

Helping you find the right solar solution for your needs and budget.

Educating communities about the benefits of solar energy.

Understand your local municipality's requirements for solar installations, including necessary permits and inspections.
